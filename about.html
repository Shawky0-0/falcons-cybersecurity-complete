About Falcons

More Than a Club — It's a Movement

Through ethical hacking, skill-building, and strong community, we aim to develop future leaders in cybersecurity. Our mission bridges the gap between academic instruction and practical industry skills.

Who We Are

Falcon's CIC is a student-led initiative dedicated to establishing a specialized cybersecurity community at Canadian International College. We empower students through hands-on, challenge-based learning, peer mentorship, and interaction with cybersecurity professionals.

Our Mission

Unite cybersecurity enthusiasts of all levels under one structured and active student community

Empower beginners through accessible learning paths, mentorship, and technical guidance

Encourage advanced students to lead workshops and mentor peers

Promote experiential learning via real-life challenges

Cultivate a culture of ethical hacking, digital awareness, and technological innovation

Our Vision

We envision a CIC community where cybersecurity awareness is deeply rooted. Within two years, Falcon's CIC will connect and empower students from diverse academic backgrounds, enabling CIC to compete in national and international cybersecurity events with pride and competence.
